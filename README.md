# Skills Link - Mitcchells Plain Local Platform
## INF3014F Individual eCommerce Project | 2026
## Project Requirements
This project was built in response to the INF3014F 2026 Individual Project Brief, which required:
- Identifying a real-world social challenge in South Africa
- Designing an e-commerce solution addressing it through a product or service
- Demonstrating entrepreneurial thinking, clear value creation, and a viable e-business model with a transactional component (book, buy, sell, or place an order)
  
## Project Overview
Townships such as Mitchells Plain are home to individuals with a wide range of valuable skills such as nail design, hairdressing, tutoring, and more, often offered at affordable prices. Despite this, many of these individuals face limited access to digital platforms that would allow them to effectively reach and grow their customer base within their own communities.

Skills Link is a proposed e-commerce platform designed to bridge this gap. The platform aims to digitalise informal skills, transforming them into sustainable economic opportunities without requiring individuals to leave their local environment. By providing a centralised, accessible space for service providers and customers to connect, the platform empowers community members to monetise their talents more efficiently.

## The problem | part 1
Informal service providers in townships like Mitchells Plain offer genuine, skilled, and affordable services. However, they largely rely on word of mouth, lacking:
- digital presence to attract new customers
- A reliable way to showcase their services and pricing
- A platform to accept bookings or orders within their community
This limits their reach, their income, and their ability to grow.

## Proposed solution and idea | part 1
An e-commerce platform purpose-built for local community service providers, enabling them to:
List their skills and services with descriptions and pricing
Receive bookings or orders from customers in the community
Build a reputation through reviews and a digital profile
Operate locally without needing to relocate or compete in external markets
