About Us

Built in Mitchell's Plain. Built for Mitchell's Plain.

Our story

Skills and Services was born out of a simple observation. Mitchell's Plain is full of talented, hardworking people whose skills never get the recognition or income they deserve. Hairdressers, electricians, tutors, plumbers, caterers; all working informally, relying on word of mouth, limited to customers on their street or in their block.

At the same time, residents looking for reliable services struggle to find people they can trust. There is no central place to search, compare, and book local help.

Skills and Services bridges that gap. We created a digital platform that gives local providers a professional presence online, and gives customers a simple, trustworthy way to find and book the help they need, within their own community.

The problem we are solving

South Africa has one of the highest unemployment rates in the world. In townships like Mitchell's Plain, youth unemployment exceeds 40%. Yet many of these unemployed residents are not without skills; they are without opportunity and visibility.

The informal economy in communities like ours is enormous, but it is invisible online. Skills and Services changes that.

What we do

For customers

Browse trusted local service providers, compare prices, and book a service online in minutes. No cash upfront, no uncertainty; just simple, reliable bookings.

For providers

Create a free profile, list your skills and pricing, and start receiving bookings from customers in your area. Build your reputation through reviews and grow your income month by month.

For the community

Every rand spent on the platform stays in the community. We are committed to building economic opportunity from within Mitchell's Plain, not importing it from outside.

Our values

Community first

We exist to serve Mitchell's Plain. Every decision we make is measured against whether it benefits the people in this community.

Trust and transparency

Customers can read reviews before booking. Nothing is hidden.

Accessibility

We keep our platform simple and mobile-friendly because we know not everyone has a laptop or fast internet at home.

Dignity in work

We believe every skill deserves respect, Our platform treats local providers as professionals, not just gig workers.
